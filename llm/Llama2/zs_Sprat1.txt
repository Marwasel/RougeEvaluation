The document is a Software Requirements Specification (SRS) for a tool called SPRAT (Security and Privacy Requirements Analysis Tool). The tool is designed to assist analysts in the scenario and goal mining, reconciliation and management processes. The SRS outlines the functional and non-functional requirements for the tool, including user access management, goal specification and management, policy management, Flesch readability index, scenario specification and management, requirements specification, legal compliance, and requirements-level access control analysis framework. The document also includes a table of contents, a glossary, an acknowledgement, and references.
