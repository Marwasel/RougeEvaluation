Summary:

The DigitalHome Software Requirements Specification document outlines the requirements for the development of a "Smart House" called DigitalHome (DH), which is a home management system that allows home residents to easily manage their daily lives by providing for a lifestyle that brings together security, environmental and energy management (temperature, humidity, and lighting), entertainment, and communications.

Requirements:

Product Description and Scope
The DigitalHome system is a web-based system that allows users to monitor and control the environment in their home.
The system is intended for use by home users and technicians.
The system will manage devices that control temperature, humidity, security, and power to small appliances and lighting units.
Users Description
The general user shall be able to use the DH system capabilities to monitor and control the environment in their home.
The general user is familiar with the layout of their home and the location of sensor and control devices.
A Master user will be designated, who shall be able to change the configuration of the system.
Development Constraints
The development team will use widely used, accepted, and available hardware and software technology and standards.
The cost of DigitalHome elements (sensors, controllers, server, tools, etc.) shall be minimized.
The system will be tested in a simulated environment.
Operational Environment
The system shall require an Internet Service Provider (ISP).
The DH Home Web Server shall provide interaction with and control of the DH elements, storage of DH plans and data, and ability to establish and maintain DH User Accounts.
The Home DH Gateway Device shall provide communication with all the DigitalHome devices and connect with a broadband Internet connection.
