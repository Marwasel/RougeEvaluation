What is the purpose of the document?
The purpose of the document is to specify the requirements for the development of a "Smart House" called DigitalHome (DH).
What is the scope of the project?
The scope of the project is to develop a limited prototype version of DigitalHome, which will be used by HomeOwner management to make business decisions about the future commercial development of DigitalHomeOwner products and services.
Who is the target audience for the document?
The target audience for the document is the DigitalHomeOwner Division, in consultation with the Marketing Division of HomeOwner Inc.
What are the main features of the DigitalHome system?
The main features of the DigitalHome system include:
Management of devices that control the environment of a home
Web-ready device for monitoring and controlling the environment
Simulated environment for testing
Ability to establish an individual home web server hosted on a home computer
Communication with all DigitalHome devices through a broadband Internet connection
What are the requirements for the development of the DigitalHome system?
The requirements for the development of the DigitalHome system include:
A "prototype" version of the DigitalHome system must be completed within twelve months of inception
The development team will consist of five engineers
The development team will use the development process specified by Digital HomeOwner Inc.
The DH system will be tested in a simulated environment
The system shall require an Internet Service Provider (ISP)
The DH Home Web Server shall provide interaction with and control of the DH elements
The DH Gateway device shall provide communication with all DigitalHome devices and shall connect with a broadband Internet connection
